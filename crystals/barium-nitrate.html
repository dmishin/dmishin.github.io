Barium nitrate

Formula: Ba(NO3)2

Properties

  • Crystal system: isometric
  • Crystal shape: truncated tetrahedorns, cubes, octahedrons
  • Color: colorless
  • Stability on air: very stable, crystals are anhydrous

Growing

As always, I used slow evaporation method to grow these crystals. Solubility of barium nitrate is merely 10.5g/100ml, quite low value for a nitrate.

Safety

All soluble barium salts are highly toxic.
